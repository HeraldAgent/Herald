# Headline Triage

Herald should separate loud news from news that can actually contaminate positioning across markets.

## Triage dimensions

- Surprise: was the headline already priced into the tape.
- Half-life: how long the market is likely to care.
- Contamination risk: how many adjacent names or sectors can inherit the impact.

## Fast rejects

- Rumors with no credible source chain.
- Headlines that only restate what participants already knew.
- Stories that move one symbol briefly but do not propagate anywhere else.

## Desired output

Each alert should explain why the story matters now and which surrounding assets need a second look.
